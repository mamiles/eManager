Purchasing License Tickets

To purchase license tickets, contact TIBCO Rendezvous Licensing (via web, email, phone or fax) and provide details of your purchase order.

Company and Contact Information

Information about your company and a contact person helps us keep in touch. We use it to send you license tickets, documentation, notices of software updates, announcements of new features, and other useful items.

  • Company name.
  • Your name (or the contact person to whom we should send all Rendezvous correspondence).
  • Your phone number (or the number of the contact person).
  • Your fax number (or the fax number of the contact person).
  • Your postal address (or the mailing address of the contact person; please include the country and postal codes as applicable).
  • Your e-mail address (or the address of the contact person).

License Information

License information helps us create the correct number of licenses, and issue appropriate license tickets.

  • Total Rendezvous licenses for each licensed Rendezvous component (for example, rvd, rvrd, rva, rvcache, rvtracer).
